Shipping and Returns

Shipping

All artworks are securely and professionally packed before shipping in either flat parcels or sturdy tubes.Shipping is charged at checkout for all products and varies depending on location.All products are sent insured, tracked and signed so you can securely follow the progress of your parcel and sign for it on arrival. Exemptions occur in Australia and Canada where either tracked or signed are only available as individual options. I usually select tracked.

Shipping Times

  • UK: usually next day but 5-7 days on large artworks.
  • Europe: 3 -7 days
  • Rest of the World: 7 - 9 days

Please note
Any customs duties or taxes incurred at customs are entirely the responsibility of the buyer.

Returns

I do not accept returns or exchanges.However if there are any problems with the order in terms of damage during shipping, a replacement will be sent free of charge.You can send a message through the 'Contact' tab or email me info@skinandcolour.com Contact page or email me at info@skinandcolour.com.
