Shipping and Returns

Shipping

All artworks are carefully and professionally packed before shipping to ensure the artwork arrives in perfect condition. All orders are shipped via tracked courier services and will require a signature upon delivery.

Shipping Times

  • UK Post: 7–10 business days
  • Rest of the World: 11–14 business days

Please note

Any customs duties or taxes incurred at customs are entirely the responsibility of the buyer.

Returns

I do not accept returns or exchanges on original artworks. However, if there are any issues regarding damage during transit, please reach out immediately via the Contact page or email me at info@skinandcolour.com.
